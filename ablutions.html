Ablutions

2018

Historically, Greek and Romans communal baths had the dual purpose of nourishing both the mind and body. But this is not the case of current showers. Post-modernism made bathing more pragmatic and showers these days became a daily routine to upkeep physical hygiene and also about minimizing water usage.

In this project, I want to reintegrate the values of baths back into the shower. To make a daily routine into a sacred ritual, physical hygiene into mindful cleansing, and bathing with a spiritual purpose. With these values, my form would follow rituals, routine and habits.
